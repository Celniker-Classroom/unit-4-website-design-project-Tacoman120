Magic: The Gathering

Magic: The Gathering is a collectable trading card game, similar to pokemon or Yu-Gi-Oh. It is renowned for it's complex strategy and deckbuilding, and is one of the most popular trading card games in the world. It doesn't matter if it is your first time playing Magic, or you've been playing throughout your life, every game of Magic is unique and entertaining.
